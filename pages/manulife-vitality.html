case studies

Manulife - Vitality Savings Tool

View live project

Manulife wanted a redesign of an existing tool that allowed users to know what rewards they could get if they joined the program. Included in the project scope was a dynamic PDF and video that compiled the user's choices and results into a personalized element.

As Lead Designer, I focused on keeping the interactivity while refreshing the look of the design to match Manulife. I also invested more time on microanimations as the theme was less formal. A fun project with many animated and moving components.

Scotia iTrade - ETF Explorer

View live project

Scotiabank requested two different user flows in one ETF finding tool. The first flow was a way to quickly filter through hundreds of ETFs in a table. The second was a questionaire where users would be presented with the best ETF options for them. Part of the project required us to design some iconography according to their brand standards to match with their existing set.

One of the first projects I worked on at Rich Media. I was given the lead UI/UX position as my experience with HTML, CSS and JS made me the ideal candidate to fulfill a quick deadline of 3 weeks.

Sonnet - Sonnet Shift Insurance Video

View official video

Sonnet commissioned a video to explain and promote their new user-based insurance initiative. Following a rising demand for live-action videos, they requested it be made with live-action footage. Creating a video featuring stock video or live-action is always difficult with a particular budget. We proposed a mix of live-action stock and animation to create a dynamic video.

As the lead video editor and designer, choosing the appropriate clips and knowing when to switch to animation was crucial. We had to deeply consider the flow and whether or not the footage fit the narrative. I also learned many tricks to better streamline my process during this project.
